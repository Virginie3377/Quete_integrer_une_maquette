hover news & quotes

by michael on october 18, 2006

Right under the navigation menu to the right you will see a heading called Recent Updates. If you hover over the news items a short summary will pop up and allow you to click to get to another page. You can use this as a link giving people a short description oh what your link is about. I learned the technique from Stu at CSS Play.The original demo was to display a small photo gallery this way but I adapted it for text.
